Greetings!

You are receiving this message because you completed New World of Work’s 21st Century Skills Training and indicated on the training feedback form that you plan to use 21st Century Skills Badges with your learners. Attached is an overview document to help you get started. It includes valuable information such as best practices for implementing 21st Century Skills Badges and the badge assessment process in LaunchPath. You’ll also find recommended resources throughout the document all of which are included in the binder and on the flash drive you received at the training. As a courtesy, all the resources are included in a DropBox folder as well, 21st Century Skills Badging- Getting Started.

The first step in accessing 21st Century Skills Badges is to create a school user account in LaunchPath. You should receive a personal invitation to register for LaunchPath soon. Use this invitation to complete your account. After completing your account, you can claim your 21st Century Skills Instructor Badge! Next, create your Mozilla Backpack using the same username as your LaunchPath account so you can push your badge from LaunchPath to your backpack and share on LinkedIn. Your own familiarity with the process will help you teach your students.

Attached is a video tutorial that will walk you through the steps of inviting your students to LaunchPath and making 21st Century Skills Badging assessments available. Please remember you need to deliver both lessons of the skill in order to prepare your students to successfully complete the badge assessment.

Please don’t hesitate to reach out if you have questions or if there’s anything I can do to support your efforts. I’m happy to help!
